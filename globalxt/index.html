    FAQ's

    Frequently Asked Questions

    We export a variety of agricultural commodities including Cowpea (Black Eyed Beans), Sesame Seeds, Cashew Nuts, Ginger, Hibiscus Flowers, Groundnuts, Tiger Nuts, and Maize.

    To place an order, contact us via email at sales@globalxtltd.com. Orders are confirmed upon receipt of a 100% irrevocable Letter of Credit (L/C) at sight from a top-tier bank.

    Shipping usually takes between 25 to 30 days after confirmation of the L/C. All cargo is shipped from Lagos Port, Nigeria with full documentation.

    Each shipment includes: Bill of Lading, Certificate of Origin, SGS or Intertek Inspection Certificate, Phytosanitary Certificate, Fumigation Certificate, Packing List, and Commercial Invoice.
